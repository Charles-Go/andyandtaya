Air and Car

The closest airport and the one we recommend is Los Angeles International (LAX).

From the airport, the Petit Ermitage Hotel is an approximately 30-minute drive.

Because of very limited parking in both West Hollywood and the beach in Santa Monica, we are providing transport from the hotel to the beach ceremony and back. We recommend using a rideshare service or a taxi from the airport. There are meet-up points for the rideshare services at each terminal, which are different from the location of taxi ranks and may be on the departures level. For details:

  • Lyft
  • Uber

The Petit Ermitage Hotel charges $42 per night for Valet parking (with in-and-out privileges), which is a similar rate to nearby garages. It is in a residential neighborhood where street parking is by permit only.

Petit Ermitage Hotel

We have asked the hotel to set aside a limited number of suites at group rates during the weekend.

Suites accommodate two adults in double, queen, or king size beds, with some adding the option to have a third person on a sofabed for an additional cost of $35/night.

Rates are between $275 and $320 plus tax and are valid until 2 May 2017. Rates include wifi, access to the heated rooftop pool, and various other amenities.

To book a room, kindly contact Eve Maltzman before May 2nd and mention the Weiss-Ledvina wedding.

Email: reservations@petitermitage.com

Phone: +1 (310) 967-6348

Child Care

The Petit Ermitage can arrange child care at the hotel with advanced notice. Please ask for assistance and information when booking. Children will be welcome at the beach ceremony but we regret not at the evening festivities, in part for safety reasons due to the layout of the roof.

Alternative Accomodation

Airbnb has many options. When searching for available listings, we recommend choosing either something close to the hotel in West Hollywood or close to the beach in Santa Monica. Please keep in mind that traffic in Los Angeles, including (sometimes especially) on weekends, can make even the most local travel very slow.
