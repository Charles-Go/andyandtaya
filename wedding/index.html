Our wedding, which will be small and intimate, is partly about introducing our close friends and family to each other. We recognize the importance of community in forging a strong marriage.

Our families love and support us and we have friends who would go to the ends of the earth for us, but because this has all happened quickly, many of them don’t know each other. We hope to change that in June and, for those in our circles who cannot attend, on our further travels.

Because our family and friends span the globe, we had a tough time choosing one place. After considering everything from Antarctica to Beirut to Cape Town, we settled on Los Angeles because it is our current home and easy for us to plan on a short timeframe.

We hope you will forgive us for not providing much lead time, as we didn't want to wait too long (crazy love!). We hope to have celebrations in South Africa and Lebanon later, and to see all of you in various places around the globe in the coming year.
