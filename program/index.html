Ceremony

Children are welcome to the ceremony. The ceremony will take place on a public beach in Santa Monica, special to us because we live nearby and enjoy walks and sunsets here. The beach has vehicle access via the Pacific Coast Highway. On foot, you can reach it by pedestrian paths involving stairs accessed at several points on Ocean Avenue and walking bridges over the PCH.

There will be three on-site coordinators who will do their best to manage the space. However, as it is a public beach, we cannot prevent beachgoers from walking past or observing. We simply hope to have them do so respectfully.

Where?

The ceremony is taking place on the public beach in front of Annenberg Beach House located at {{ site.data.places.beach.address }}. Transportation will be provided from the hotel to the beach and back.

{% include map.html q=site.data.places.beach.q %}

There is a long pathway which leads right up to the shore where the ceremony will take place. The ceremony itself is set up on the sand, and there will be a place to leave your shoes if you don’t want to carry them.

There is a parking lot indicated in the photo above for those who drive on their own. All beach parking tends to fill up on weekends, so please plan to arrive early. From the parking lot, make your way towards the ocean and you will see the pathway.

If you are early, the Back on the Beach Cafe is located in the spot where the pathway begins so you can have a drink or a snack while you wait.
